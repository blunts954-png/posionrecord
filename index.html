Why Poison Well Records Is The Best

01

Real Limited Pressings

When we say limited, we mean it. Our vinyl runs are genuinely small batch — not manufactured scarcity. Once they're gone, they're gone forever. No represses, no second chances. That's how real collectors know we respect the game.

02

805 Scene Authenticity

We're not carpetbaggers. We're based in Ventura, breathing the same air as the bands we release. We know the venues, the basements, the back rooms where this music was born. Every release is a document of real California punk history.

03

Archive Quality Restoration

Our archival releases aren't just transferred — they're restored with care. Narthex Structure's 1984 recordings, Dr. Know's CBGB set, The Missing 23rd's classics — we treat these like the historical documents they are.

04

No Corporate BS

Zero algorithms. Zero playlist optimization. Zero industry middlemen taking their cut. When you buy from Poison Well, your money goes to the artists and the label — not some streaming platform's shareholders.

05

Worldwide Shipping

From Ventura to Vienna, Tokyo to Toronto — we ship our vinyl everywhere punks live. Sturdy mailers, proper packaging, and records that arrive in the condition they left. We've shipped to 30+ countries and counting.

06

Community First

We're not just a store — we're part of the 805 punk community. We sponsor shows, support local venues, and keep the scene alive. Every record you buy helps fund the next release, the next show, the next piece of history.

What Is Poison Well Records?

Poison Well Records is a small, stubbornly independent label focused on loud, strange, and emotional music — punk, post‑punk, metal, hardcore, whatever feels honest.

We're rooted in the California DIY scene: tiny rooms, blown-out PAs, friends doing the door, bands hauling their own gear.

The label exists to document that energy on vinyl and tape, not to smooth it out for playlists or algorithms.

If you've ever bought a record at a show, read the liner notes front to back, and cared who pressed it, you're in the right place.

Shipping, Grading & Returns

We ship from California using sturdy mailers, padded inserts, and common sense. Most orders go out within 2–5 business days.

New records are sold as‑new, straight from the plant or our boxes. Used records are visually graded using standard Goldmine-style grades (VG, VG+, etc.), and we don't overgrade.

If something shows up trashed from the mail or not as described, email us with photos within 7 days — we'll replace it if we can, or refund you if we can't.

All online checkout is handled through Bandcamp and/or Discogs for secure payment and buyer protection.

Stay in the Well

We don't spam. We send emails when there's a new record, a preorder that might sell out, or a run of tapes we don't have the energy to promote anywhere else.

Drop your email and you'll hear about limited runs, weird variants, and exclusive leftovers before they vanish into Discogs price‑gouge land.
